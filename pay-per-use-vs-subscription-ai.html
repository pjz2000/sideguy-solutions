Pay-Per-Use vs Subscription AI

Subscription AI charges continuously. Pay-per-use AI charges only when work runs.

What problem this solves

Many businesses pay monthly for tools they barely touch.

How pay-per-use differs

  • No ongoing fees
  • Costs tied to actual jobs
  • Easier to stop or change

Where humans stay involved

Humans decide what runs and review outcomes.

When pay-per-use makes sense

Irregular or experimental workloads.

When subscriptions make sense

High-volume, constant usage.
