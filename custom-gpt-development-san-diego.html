Common mistakes

What’s the difference between a custom GPT and a regular chatbot?

Custom GPTs are trained or prompted with your specific knowledge and have persistent context. They behave like a colleague who knows your business, not a generic assistant.

How is my data kept private?

Via OpenAI’s enterprise API or self-hosted models. We architect for data privacy based on your compliance requirements before any build.

Do I need to keep it updated?

Yes. A GPT is only as good as its training data. We build update workflows into every deployment.

What’s the first step?

Identify the single highest-value knowledge gap — the question your team answers most that doesn’t require human judgment. That’s the first GPT use case.
