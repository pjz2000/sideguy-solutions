How to Find a Licensed Contractor in Leucadia

Clarity before selling: Use this checklist to verify contractors—always confirm licenses and insurance before work starts.

What to check

  • Active license number with the CA Contractors State License Board
  • General liability and workers' comp insurance
  • Local references and recent work photos

Interview questions

  1. How long have you worked locally?
  2. Can you provide recent references and photos?
  3. Who will be on site and subcontracted?

Permits and inspections

Confirm who will pull permits and check local inspection requirements for your project.

FAQ

Should I get multiple bids? Yes—3 bids gives context; compare scope, timeline, and materials, not just price.
