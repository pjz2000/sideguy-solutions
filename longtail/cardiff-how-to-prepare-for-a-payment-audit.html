How to Prepare for a Payment Audit (Cardiff Merchants)

Clarity before selling: Steps to gather the right records and reduce surprises during a payment processor or bank audit.

Records to gather

  • Processing statements (3–12 months)
  • Charge receipts and invoices
  • Refund, void, and dispute logs

Common auditor requests

Auditors commonly request merchant agreements, chargeback details, and proof of goods/services delivered.

How to streamline

  1. Create a single folder with requested docs
  2. Prepare concise answers for recurring questions
  3. Assign a single contact to manage auditor communications

FAQ

How long does an audit take? Typically 1–4 weeks depending on complexity and how quickly you provide records.
