When to Use On-Chain Settlements for Payments (Leucadia)

Clarity before selling: On-chain settlements can help with settlement speed and transparency but add complexity and fees—evaluate carefully.

When it makes sense

  • High-value cross-border payments
  • Use cases that need tamper-proof settlement records

Tradeoffs

Costs, volatility, and regulatory considerations can make on-chain less attractive for small merchants.

FAQ

Are on-chain settlements instant? Finality varies by chain and confirmations—some are fast, others can take minutes to hours depending on the network.
