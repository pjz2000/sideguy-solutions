How to Qualify for Small Business Energy Rebates in Solana Beach

Clarity before selling: This guide explains typical eligibility rules and documentation for small business energy rebates—verify specifics with your utility or program admin.

Common eligibility

  • Business size and NAICS codes sometimes apply
  • Proof of address and business registration required

How to apply

  1. Check the utility or county rebate portal
  2. Collect invoices and proof of purchase
  3. Submit application or work through a participating contractor

FAQ

How long does processing take? Typically 4–12 weeks depending on the program and paperwork.
