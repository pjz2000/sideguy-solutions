How tokenized payments work — simple explanation

SideGuy: Clarity before selling — this page explains what changed, why it’s confusing, what can break if ignored, and neutral options.

What changed (facts only)

Tokenization became more common as a security and routing mechanism for both off-chain and tokenized rails.

Why this confused people

Different vendors call tokens different things and the trust model varies by implementation.

What breaks if you ignore it

Failed refunds, improper token refresh handling, and reconciliation gaps.

What options exist now (neutral)

Use tokenization for PCI scope reduction and for marketplace splits with clear reconciliation processes.
