What to do if your payment processor raises fees

SideGuy: Clarity before selling — what changed, why it’s confusing, what breaks, and neutral options.

What changed (facts only)

  • Processor fees were adjusted for some merchants and some programs introduced new components or surcharges.

Why this confused people

Fee notices can be buried in terms and dashboards; merchants don’t always receive clear examples or calculations.

What breaks if you ignore it

Unexpected margin compression and cashflow problems.

What options exist now (neutral)

Ask for a sample statement, negotiate, compare processors, or switch pricing models; get a short fee audit before moving.
