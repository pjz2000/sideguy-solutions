How to Avoid AC Freezing Up (Cardiff)

Clarity before selling: Simple seasonal maintenance reduces AC freeze events—this guide covers checks and when to call a pro.

Checklist

  • Replace or clean filters monthly
  • Ensure proper airflow and open vents
  • Keep refrigerant at correct levels (pro check)

When to call a pro

If the unit repeatedly freezes after defrosting, schedule a licensed HVAC tech to check for leaks or airflow issues.

FAQ

Can I run the unit after defrosting? It’s fine briefly to defrost, but avoid prolonged operation if ice returns—call a technician.
