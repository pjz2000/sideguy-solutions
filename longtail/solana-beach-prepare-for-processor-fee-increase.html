How to Prepare for a Payment Processor Fee Increase (Solana Beach)

Clarity before selling: Practical steps to audit fees, negotiate, and evaluate switching costs when your processor raises rates.

Immediate actions

  1. Export 3–6 months of processing statements
  2. Calculate the monthly impact of the increase
  3. Contact your rep with volume and ask for alternatives

Alternatives

Consider interchange-plus pricing, alternative processors, or surcharging (where legal).

Next steps

Run a short quote comparison and estimate migration time and costs before deciding.

FAQ

How quickly can I switch? Small merchants can switch in 1–2 weeks but expect reconciliation work and possible gaps in reporting for the first month.
