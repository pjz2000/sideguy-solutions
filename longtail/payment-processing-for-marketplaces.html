Payment processing for marketplaces

SideGuy: Clarity before selling — this page documents what changed, why it's confusing, and neutral options; it is informational, not a sales pitch.

What changed (facts only)

More platforms provide split-settlement, marketplace SDKs, and easier vendor onboarding; however, fee and liability models vary widely.

Why this confused people

Marketplace operators must choose between acting as a money transmitter or using managed payouts; terminology and obligations create confusion.

What breaks if you ignore it

Poorly designed flows can create liability, misallocated payouts, and customer disputes across buyers and sellers.

What options exist now (neutral)

  • Use marketplace-aware payment providers that support split payouts and compliance tools.
  • Consider hosted vendor onboarding to reduce compliance burden.
