How to implement instant payouts

SideGuy: Clarity before selling — this page documents what changed, why it's confusing, and neutral options; it is informational, not a sales pitch.

What changed (facts only)

Several providers now offer instant or near-instant payouts using new rails; these come with fee and eligibility trade-offs.

Why this confused people

Advertised "instant" often requires account eligibility checks and may not be available to all merchant types or currencies.

What breaks if you ignore it

Unexpected fees and payout holds can hurt cashflow planning; selection without testing can create reconciliation problems.

What options exist now (neutral)

  • Test with a small subset of payout flows and compare fees for true net cash impact.
  • Negotiate eligibility and fee access with providers before switching on broadly.
