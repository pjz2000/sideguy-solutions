AI cost estimation for small businesses

SideGuy: Clarity before selling — this page documents what changed, why it's confusing, and neutral options; it is informational, not a sales pitch.

What changed (facts only)

Cloud AI pricing has matured; however, total project cost includes data work, integration, monitoring, and potential subscription model fees.

Why this confused people

Vendors quote per-inference or per-token pricing but integration and maintenance costs often dominate small projects.

What breaks if you ignore it

Under-budgeted pilots can fail mid-project when integration and monitoring costs surface unexpectedly.

What options exist now (neutral)

  • Estimate engineering hours for integration and monitoring, not just model fees.
  • Start small with a scoped pilot and measure OPEX before scaling.
