How to Use UTM Tracking for Local Campaigns (Leucadia)

Clarity before selling: A simple UTM scheme helps measure which local campaigns drive visits—keep names consistent and documented.

UTM naming tips

  • Use utm_source, utm_medium, utm_campaign consistently
  • Include town or neighborhood in utm_campaign for local clarity

Quick analysis

Check sessions, conversions, and landing page behavior in analytics to measure ROI on local efforts.

FAQ

How should I name campaigns? Example: utm_source=facebook&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=leucadia_summer_sale
