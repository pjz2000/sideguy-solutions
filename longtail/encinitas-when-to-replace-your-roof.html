When to Replace vs Repair Your Roof (Encinitas)

Clarity before selling: Assess leaks, age, and repeated repairs when deciding to replace a roof—consult local roofers for accurate quotes.

Signs you may need replacement

  • Multiple leaks or widespread shingle loss
  • Roof age >20–25 years depending on materials

Cost considerations

Obtain 2–3 quotes, ask about tear-off vs overlay, and check warranties on materials and labor.

FAQ

Should I get multiple inspections? Yes—3 quotes helps compare scope, not just price.
