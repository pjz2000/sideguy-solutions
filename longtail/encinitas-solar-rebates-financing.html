Solar Rebates & Financing for Encinitas Homeowners

Clarity before selling: Evaluate rebates, federal tax credits, and financing options—compare total cost, not monthly payment alone.

Rebates & credits

Check federal tax credits, state incentives, and local utility rebates. Eligibility varies by household income and equipment.

Financing options

  • Cash purchase (lowest long-term cost)
  • Loan financing
  • Leases and PPA (less common for homeowners)

Installer checklist

  1. Verify licenses and backup references
  2. Ask for detailed production estimates and warranties
  3. Confirm rebate application process

FAQ

Do I qualify for federal tax credit? Most homeowners do; consult a tax advisor for details and eligibility.
