Best Practices to Prevent Fraud for Small San Diego Merchants

Clarity before selling: Low-effort checks and tools can materially reduce fraud—start with simple rules and monitor transactions.

Quick wins

  • Enable AVS and CVV checks for card-not-present transactions
  • Use velocity rules to block suspicious behavior
  • Keep clear refund policies and receipts

When to add tools

Use fraud scoring and manual review for high-value or repeat transactions.

FAQ

What is a velocity rule? A rule that limits the number of transactions or attempts in a time window to reduce automated fraud attempts.
