How to collect online payments safely — best practices for merchants

SideGuy: Clarity before selling — what changed, why it’s confusing, what breaks, and neutral options.

What changed (facts only)

  • More turnkey payment integrations and hosted checkout options reduce PCI scope for merchants.

Why this confused people

Different vendors offer different levels of scope reduction and claims can be unclear.

What breaks if you ignore it

Exposure to card data, increased PCI burden, and regulatory risk.

What options exist now (neutral)

Use hosted checkout or tokenization, ensure TLS, and request an SAQ from your vendor.
