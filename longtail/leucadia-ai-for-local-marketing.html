AI for Local Marketing: A Simple Guide for Leucadia Small Businesses

Clarity before selling: This is a pragmatic overview—test small and measure real customer actions before committing to bigger AI projects.

Where to start

Start with content generation for local listings and short ad copy tests; focus on actions that drive bookings or visits.

Tools to try

  • AI headline & description generators for GMB and ads
  • Simple local audience ad tests with small budgets

Quick test plan

  1. Define one conversion metric (call, booking, direction clicks)
  2. Run two ad variants for 1–2 weeks
  3. Measure and scale the winner

Risks & tips

Start small, watch for hallucinated content and verify local references. Prefer human review for customer-facing text.

FAQ

How much should a small test cost? $50–$200 over two weeks is a good starting point for local ads and content tests.
