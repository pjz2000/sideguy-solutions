Payment processing comparison — a quick guide for San Diego merchants

SideGuy: Clarity before selling — this page explains what changed, why it confuses people, what can break if ignored, and neutral options.

What changed (facts only)

  • Pricing models and payout options diversified (interchange+pass-through, flat-rate, instant payouts).

Why this confused people

Statement formats differ and processors use different line items for fees and adjustments.

What breaks if you ignore it

Unexpected fees and reconciliation headaches that reduce margins.

What options exist now (neutral)

Ask for a sample statement, request line-by-line fee explanations, and compare settlement timing.
