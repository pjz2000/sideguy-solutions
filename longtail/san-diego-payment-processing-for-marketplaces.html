Payment Processing Options for Marketplaces in San Diego

Clarity before selling: Marketplace payments vary by model—split payouts, compliance, and fees differ; this is an informational guide.

Quick overview

Marketplaces can use managed payout providers, platform processors, or split-pay systems; choose by scale, risk, and complexity.

Common models

  • Platform-as-merchant (processor handles settlements)
  • Managed payouts (provider splits funds to sellers)
  • On-chain settlements for niche use cases

What to watch for

Fee structure, chargeback routing, KYC/AML obligations, and how refunds are handled across sellers.

Checklist before choosing

  1. Map flows for payouts and refunds
  2. Confirm reporting and reconciliation capabilities
  3. Check seller onboarding KYC requirements

Next steps

Run a short technical feasibility test with a sandbox account and validate reconciliation reports for 2–3 sample transactions.

FAQ

Do marketplaces need separate legal entities for sellers?
Usually sellers remain independent; some models require platform-level compliance—check with counsel and your processor.
Can I offer instant payouts to sellers?
Yes, via instant payout partners, but expect higher per-transaction fees and additional KYC checks.
