How to Prep for an Appliance Repair Visit (Leucadia)

Clarity before selling: A short prep checklist to speed diagnostics and reduce visit time: clear work area, capture serial numbers, and have purchase info ready.

Quick prep steps

  1. Clear the area and remove nearby items
  2. Have model and serial numbers ready
  3. Take photos or video of the issue if possible

FAQ

Should I unplug the appliance? Only if safe to do so; otherwise wait for the technician to advise.
