What to Do If Your Payment Processor Raises Fees (Encinitas)

Clarity before selling: Practical advice to evaluate whether to negotiate or switch; consult a payments expert for complex contracts.

Immediate checklist

  • Identify exact fee changes and effective date
  • Export 3 months of processing statements
  • Calculate the monthly impact

Negotiate with evidence

Compare alternatives, present your volume, and ask for rate reductions or interchange-plus pricing.

Alternatives

  • Negotiate merchant rate
  • Switch to an interchange-plus processor
  • Consider surcharging or increasing minimums where legal

FAQs

Is it worth switching? It depends—calculate break-even including migration and downtime costs.
