How to prepare for a payment audit — checklist for merchants

SideGuy: Clarity before selling — what changed, why it’s confusing, what breaks, and neutral options.

What changed (facts only)

  • Audits now look for stronger reconciliation trails and clearer documentation for chargebacks and refunds.

Why this confused people

Merchants are often unsure which documents to include or how to format reconciliation evidence.

What breaks if you ignore it

Failed audits, fines, or extended account reviews that affect settlements.

What options exist now (neutral)

Keep organized statement archives, reconciliation reports, and a chargeback log; ask your processor for audit guidance.
