Cryptocurrency payments — pros and cons for merchants

SideGuy: Clarity before selling — what changed, why it’s confusing, what breaks, and neutral options.

What changed (facts only)

  • Tools to accept crypto payments and settle to fiat have become more available, but custody and volatility remain concerns.

Why this confused people

Terms like 'instant settlement' or 'zero fees' often mask conversion costs and custody responsibilities.

What breaks if you ignore it

Unexpected FX losses, reconciliation mismatch, and regulatory questions for business accounting.

What options exist now (neutral)

Use vetted payment processors that handle immediate fiat conversion, or accept crypto for niche cases with clear accounting policies.
