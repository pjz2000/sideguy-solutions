How to Avoid Contractor Scams (Solana Beach)

Clarity before selling: Protect yourself—verify licenses, ask for references, and avoid large upfront cash payments to reduce scam risk.

Red flags

  • No license or insurance proof
  • Asks for large upfront cash deposits
  • No written contract or specifics on materials

FAQ

What if I suspect a scam? Stop payments if possible, document communications, and file a complaint with local authorities and the Contractors State License Board.
