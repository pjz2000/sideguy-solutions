Simple Ways to Reduce Payment Declines for Card-present Sales (San Diego)

Clarity before selling: Small terminal and process changes reduce declines—train staff, keep terminals updated, and use EMV readers.

Quick fixes

  • Use EMV-capable terminals and keep firmware updated
  • Check card readability and train staff on chip/card insertion
  • Implement manual entry rules carefully and avoid errors

FAQ

Do new terminals reduce declines? Often yes—modern terminals handle card reads and fallback better than older units.
