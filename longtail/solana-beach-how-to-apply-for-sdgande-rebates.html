How to Apply for SDG&E Rebates in Solana Beach

Clarity before selling: This is an informational guide—verify eligibility with SDG&E or the program administrator.

Overview

SDG&E runs periodic rebate programs for energy-efficient appliances, heat pumps, and home battery storage. Start by checking SDG&E's rebate portal.

Eligibility

  • Programs have income caps or equipment-specific requirements
  • Must be installed by a participating contractor for some rebates

How to apply

  1. Check SDG&E rebate portal and confirm program availability
  2. Collect invoices and product specs
  3. Submit online or through your contractor

Expected timing

Processing can take 4–12 weeks depending on program and paperwork completeness.

FAQ

Do I need a licensed installer? Some rebates require a participating contractor—check program rules.
