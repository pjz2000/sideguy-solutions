AI for payment fraud detection — what merchants need to know

SideGuy: Clarity before selling — what changed, why it’s confusing, what breaks, and neutral options.

What changed (facts only)

  • AI models are increasingly used to flag suspicious transactions and automate review queues.

Why this confused people

Score thresholds and vendor tuning vary; merchants don’t always see the model reasoning behind declines.

What breaks if you ignore it

Higher false declines that lead to lost sales or unresolved fraud losses.

What options exist now (neutral)

Work with your processor to tune thresholds, add manual review for edge cases, and use chargeback alerts.
