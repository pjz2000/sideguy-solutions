Key Questions to Ask Before Signing a Payment Contract (San Diego)

Clarity before selling: Check fees, termination terms, and reporting before you sign—small clauses can have big cost impacts.

Must-ask questions

  • What is the exact pricing model and effective rate?
  • Are there early termination fees or minimum terms?
  • Who owns the merchant account and settlement access?

Red flags

Unclear chargeback handling, vague fees, and no clear statement format are warning signs.

FAQ

Can I negotiate these terms? Often yes—share your volume and ask for clear interchange-plus pricing or concessions.
