Why Is My AC Freezing Up (Solana Beach)

Clarity before selling: Troubleshooting steps to try, with advice on when to call a licensed HVAC technician.

Common causes

  • Low refrigerant
  • Poor airflow (dirty filters)
  • Thermostat or control issues

Quick checks

  1. Replace or clean filters
  2. Ensure vents are open
  3. Check for ice buildup and turn off system to defrost

When to call a pro

If the unit keeps freezing after basic checks, call a licensed HVAC technician for refrigerant and system diagnostics.

FAQ

Will adding freon fix it? Only if a leak is not present—leaks must be found and repaired first.
