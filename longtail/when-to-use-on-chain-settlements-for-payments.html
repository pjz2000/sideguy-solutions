When to use on-chain settlements for payments

SideGuy: Clarity before selling — this page documents what changed, why it's confusing, and neutral options; it is informational, not a sales pitch.

What changed (facts only)

Adoption of on-chain settlement options increased in niche cases, with faster finality on some chains and new stablecoin rails that reduce volatility risk for merchants.

Why this confused people

The technical terms and custody choices (custodial vs self-custody) confuse merchant decisions and integration requirements.

What breaks if you ignore it

Poorly chosen rails can add reconciliation complexity, tax and compliance needs, and potential conversion costs back to fiat.

What options exist now (neutral)

  • Consider on-chain for international micro-payments or where instant settlement matters.
  • Use stablecoins with trusted custody partners to reduce volatility.
  • Evaluate compliance and tax implications before adoption.
