How to Switch Payment Providers in San Diego Without Downtime

Clarity before selling: This is an operational guide—get a sandbox environment, run test transactions, and schedule the final cutover during low traffic.

Plan and timeline

Plan a week of testing, staging integration, and a low-traffic cutover window. Keep both processors running in parallel during testing.

Steps

  1. Test in sandbox with full payment flows
  2. Validate refunds, chargebacks, subscription renewals
  3. Prepare customer-facing communications

Common pitfalls

Not testing refunds or recurring payments, and switching during peak hours. Validate webhook handling and reconciliation reports in parallel.

After cutover

Monitor settlement reports, reconcile closely for the first 7–14 days, and be ready to rollback if major issues appear.

FAQ

Can I do this in one weekend? For some setups yes; however complex subscription and marketplace platforms usually need staged rollout and reconciliation time.
