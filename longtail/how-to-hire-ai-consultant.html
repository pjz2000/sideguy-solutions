How to hire an AI consultant — checklist for small businesses

SideGuy: Clarity before selling — document what changed, why it’s confusing, what can break, and neutral options before committing to a vendor.

What changed (facts only)

  • Increased vendor offerings and templated POCs for common small-business problems.

Why this confused people

Vendors use different definitions of success and vary on data handling terms.

What breaks if you ignore it

Unclear deliverables and surprises about data access or ownership.

What options exist now (neutral)

Smaller pilot contracts, simple KPIs, and staged payments tied to deliverables.

If this affected you, text PJ

Text PJ

  • Define the problem and success metrics clearly.
  • Ask for a short proof-of-concept with fixed scope and cost.
  • Check data access, privacy, and who will own final outputs.
  • Prefer staged contracts with small milestones.
