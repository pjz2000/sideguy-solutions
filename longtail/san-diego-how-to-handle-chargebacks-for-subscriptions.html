How to Handle Chargebacks for Subscription Services (San Diego)

Clarity before selling: Subscription chargebacks need clear records of signup, consent, and a clear cancel policy—gather evidence quickly and respond within time windows.

Key evidence

  • Signed terms or checkbox consent
  • IP/device info and transaction receipts
  • Communication records about cancellations/refunds

Prevention tips

  1. Send clear confirmation emails with plan, next bill date, and cancellation instructions
  2. Keep logs for trial-to-paid conversions

FAQ

Do trials increase chargeback risk? Slightly—confirming intent and collecting payment method upfront reduces abuse.
