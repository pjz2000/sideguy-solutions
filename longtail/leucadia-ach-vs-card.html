How to Choose Between ACH and Card Payments (Leucadia)

Clarity before selling: Compare fees, settlement times, and dispute risk before choosing ACH or card for your business model.

When to use ACH

  • Low-cost recurring payments and large B2B transfers
  • Slower settlement but lower fees than cards

When to use cards

  • Fast settlement and better for one-time consumer payments
  • Higher fees and greater chargeback risk

FAQ

Can I accept both? Yes—many businesses use cards for instant capture and ACH for larger or recurring B2B payments.
