How to read your payment processor statement — a simple guide

SideGuy: Clarity before selling — what changed, why it’s confusing, what breaks, and neutral options.

What changed (facts only)

  • Statement layouts vary widely and new adjustment types (e.g., network fees) are appearing more often.

Why this confused people

Line items aren’t standardized and terminology differs by processor.

What breaks if you ignore it

Missed fees and reconciliations that reduce profit or lead to billing errors.

What options exist now (neutral)

Request a sample statement, get a short line-by-line explanation from support, and compare to prior months.
