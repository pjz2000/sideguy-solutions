How to Add Instant Payouts for Local Delivery Drivers (San Diego)

Clarity before selling: Instant payouts help drivers but come with fees and KYC requirements—this note covers typical options and tradeoffs.

How it works

Providers offer on-demand payouts via debit push or bank transfers; funds reach drivers in minutes but cost more per payout.

Checklist

  1. Decide payout cadence and caps
  2. Confirm KYC/AML requirements for drivers
  3. Estimate fees and net impact on margins

FAQ

Are instant payouts available for all banks? Most major banks support push payouts via providers; some smaller banks may delay or charge additional fees.
