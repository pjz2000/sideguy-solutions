Understanding interchange fees for small businesses

SideGuy: Clarity before selling — this page documents what changed, why it's confusing, and neutral options; it is informational, not a sales pitch.

What changed (facts only)

Interchange rules and categories continue to be the dominant cost for card acceptance; nuance in card types affects merchant rates materially.

Why this confused people

Merchant statements combine interchange and processing marks, making it hard to see the underlying interchange costs.

What breaks if you ignore it

Ignoring interchange structure can leave money on the table and cause surprise costs during peak seasons or with certain payment types.

What options exist now (neutral)

  • Review interchange categories with your processor and ask for examples for your industry.
  • Consider routing or ACH for large or recurring payments when appropriate.
