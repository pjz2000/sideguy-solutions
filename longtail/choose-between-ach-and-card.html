Choose between ACH and card payments — when to use each

SideGuy: Clarity before selling — what changed, why it’s confusing, what breaks, and neutral options.

What changed (facts only)

  • ACH rails matured for recurring billing, offering lower per-transaction costs but different dispute and return risks.

Why this confused people

Fee comparisons can be misleading because ACH tends to have different risk and return behaviors compared to cards.

What breaks if you ignore it

Unexpected returns, slower settlement, or cashflow mismatches for businesses used to card timing.

What options exist now (neutral)

Use ACH for high-value or recurring B2B charges; use card for smaller, consumer transactions; consider mixed strategies for net cost optimization.
