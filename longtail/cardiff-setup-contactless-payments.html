How to Set Up Contactless Payments for a Small Cafe in Cardiff

Clarity before selling: Contactless improves speed—compare fees, terminal compatibility, and staff training before purchasing devices.

Hardware choices

  • Countertop terminals vs mobile card readers
  • EMV & tap-to-pay support recommended

Start-up checklist

  1. Confirm payment provider supports your POS
  2. Test transaction flow during quiet hours
  3. Train staff on quick tap handling and receipts

FAQ

Do contactless payments cost more? They can have similar fees to cards; competition between providers helps keep costs reasonable.
