Blockchain payment processing explained — tokenized payments & rails

SideGuy: Clarity before selling — this page explains what changed, why it’s confusing, what can break if ignored, and neutral options.

What changed (facts only)

  • More tokenization and on-chain settlement experiments emerged for marketplaces and receipts.

Why this confused people

Messaging around blockchain benefits often conflates proofs-of-concept with production-ready payments rails.

What breaks if you ignore it

Poor reconciliation, unclear custody responsibilities, and unexpected on/off-ramps fees.

What options exist now (neutral)

Use tokenization for receipts or settlement proofs only when you have clear reconciliation paths and vendor guarantees.

If this affected you, text PJ

Text PJ

Blockchain can help with settlement transparency, tokenized receipts, and programmable payouts, but it’s not a free substitute for regulated payment rails.

When blockchain helps

  • Programmable payouts for marketplaces
  • Immutable receipts for disputes
  • Cross-border settlements in tokenized form (with fiat off-ramps)
