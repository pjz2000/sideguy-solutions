Blockchain payment processing explained — tokenized payments & rails

Blockchain can help with settlement transparency, tokenized receipts, and programmable payouts, but it’s not a free substitute for regulated payment rails.

When blockchain helps

  • Programmable payouts for marketplaces
  • Immutable receipts for disputes
  • Cross-border settlements in tokenized form (with fiat off-ramps)
