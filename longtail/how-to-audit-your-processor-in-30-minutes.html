How to audit your payment processor in 30 minutes

SideGuy: Clarity before selling — this page documents what changed, why it's confusing, and neutral options; it is informational, not a sales pitch.

What changed (facts only)

Statement line-items, interchange flows, and payout timings are more granular; many merchants miss line-item differences that matter to margins.

Why this confused people

Terms like interchange, assessment, and pass-through add complexity; statements vary by provider and require a line-by-line glance.

What breaks if you ignore it

Unexpected fees hurt margins and cashflow; missing fee categories when comparing quotes can lead to poor provider choices.

What options exist now (neutral)

  • Compare a single representative month across providers.
  • Ask for a sample merchant statement with detailed interchange breakdown.
  • Consider an independent audit if statements are unreadable.
