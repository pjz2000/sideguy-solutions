How to Handle Chargebacks Efficiently (Leucadia)

Clarity before selling: Document and respond quickly—collected evidence increases the chance of winning disputes and reducing costs.

Quick steps

  1. Collect receipts, shipping/proof of service, and communications
  2. Submit evidence within processor time windows
  3. Follow up with clear notes and contact info

Prevention

  • Clear refund policies
  • Delivery tracking where appropriate
  • Strong customer service and quick resolution

FAQ

What counts as good evidence? Proof of service/delivery, clear receipts, and customer communications showing agreement to the charge.
