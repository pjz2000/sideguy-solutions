Contractor ghosted me — what to do in San Diego

SideGuy: Clarity before selling — what changed, why it’s confusing, what breaks, and neutral options.

What changed (facts only)

  • Reports of contractor availability combined with some companies failing to finish jobs increased complaints in the region.

Why this confused people

Promises, partial work, and unclear warranties make it hard to know remedy steps.

What breaks if you ignore it

Partial or unsafe repairs, lien risks, and added costs to rehire or remediate.

What options exist now (neutral)

Document everything, request contract terms in writing, check licensing, get local references, and consult small claims/licensing boards if needed.
