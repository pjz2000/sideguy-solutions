# SIDEGUY CORE DOCTRINE

## What SideGuy Is
SideGuy is a calm, human-first clarity layer for people making stressful decisions.

## What SideGuy Is Not
- Not a sales funnel
- Not a SaaS product
- Not a consultant pitch
- Not urgency-driven
- Not built to pressure people

## The One Rule
Offer the truth. Nothing more.

## How SideGuy Helps
- Explains reality plainly
- Reduces unnecessary stress
- Helps people avoid bad decisions
- Leaves control with the person reading

## When Humans Get Involved
Only when clarity alone isn’t enough.

If we can help, great.
If not, that’s okay too.
