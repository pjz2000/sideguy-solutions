Common questions

What is the safest way to store crypto?

Hardware wallets (Ledger, Trezor) keep private keys offline — safest for large amounts. For day-to-day spending, a software wallet (Phantom for Solana, MetaMask for Ethereum) is fine. Never store seed phrases digitally.

What happens if I lose my seed phrase?

Your funds are permanently inaccessible. Seed phrases are the only way to recover a wallet. Store them written on paper in a fireproof safe, or use a steel backup plate.

Can a business accept crypto payments without volatility risk?

Yes — accept stablecoins (USDC, USDT) in your wallet and they maintain a 1:1 USD peg. Solana Pay makes USDC acceptance as simple as a QR code with near-zero fees.
