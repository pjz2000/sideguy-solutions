Common questions

What is the average credit card processing fee for small business?

Typical rates range from 1.5% to 3.5% per transaction depending on card type and processor. Flat-rate processors like Square charge ~2.6% + $0.10 for in-person swipes.

How do I lower my payment processing fees?

Negotiate interchange-plus pricing (vs flat rate), encourage debit card payments, batch transactions daily, and avoid manually-keyed entries which cost more.

What is the fastest way to get paid as a small business?

Instant ACH transfer (same-day) or Solana stablecoin settlement (under 1 second). Both beat the standard 1–2 business day card funding window.
