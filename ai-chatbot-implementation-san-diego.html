Where operators get stuck

Website visitors leave without engaging

Most small business websites convert under 2% of visitors. A well-configured chatbot that asks the right qualifying questions can significantly improve capture rate for high-intent visitors.

Staff answer the same 10 questions repeatedly

FAQ-qualified chatbots (hours, pricing, service areas, booking links) reduce inbound contact volume for questions that don’t need human attention.

Lead handoff is slow and inconsistent

Chatbot-to-CRM handoff automation ensures every qualified lead is logged, tagged, and followed up on — without relying on manual copy-paste.

Common mistakes

What platform should I build on?

Depends on your website and CRM. Common options: Tidio, Intercom, Voiceflow, or custom GPT-backed bots. We match the platform to your actual use case.

How long does chatbot implementation take?

Simple FAQ bots: 1–2 weeks. Complex qualification flows with CRM integration: 3–6 weeks.

What if the chatbot gets something wrong?

Design matters more than AI capability. Well-scoped bots with clear fallback to human handoff outperform over-ambitious bots every time.

What’s the first step?

Document the 10 most common questions your team answers and your current lead capture flow. That defines the chatbot’s job.
