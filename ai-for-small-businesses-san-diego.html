AI for small businesses — quick wins and practical steps

A short primer for local businesses: where to look for quick wins, how to scope pilots, and realistic expectations about costs and outcomes.

Where to look for opportunities

  • Customer messages and triage
  • Invoice and payment workflows
  • Basic forecasting and ordering suggestions

Quick wins

Start with small problems that are high-volume and low-risk—automating replies or routing tasks can yield fast wins.

How to pick a pilot

Pick a task with clear baseline metrics, available sample data, and minimal regulatory concerns.

Costs & timeline

Expect pilots to cost a few thousand dollars and run for 2–6 weeks; include conservative estimates for ongoing usage.

FAQ

Can I run a pilot without hiring an ML team?
Yes—pilots can be run with a small cross-functional team and outside contractor support if needed.
