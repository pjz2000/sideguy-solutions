Do I need to be tech-savvy to get help?

Not at all. We explain everything in plain English. If you can text us what's wrong, we can help — no technical knowledge required.

How fast do you respond?

Usually within a few minutes during San Diego business hours. Text is fastest — 773-544-1231.

Will you try to sell me something?

No. Our job is giving you honest information. If you genuinely need a paid service or vendor, we'll tell you who and what to expect to pay — nothing more.

My internet/WiFi keeps dropping — can you actually fix that?

We can diagnose it. Most WiFi drops come from a handful of causes — we'll walk you through the checklist and tell you if it's your router, your ISP, or your house wiring.

Can you help my elderly parent with their phone or tablet?

Yes — this is something we do a lot. We can walk through setup, safety settings, and scam protection over text or call. Patient and judgment-free.

I got a scary message saying my computer has a virus — is it real?

Almost certainly a scam. Don't call any number in the message, don't click anything. Text us the exact wording and we'll confirm within minutes.
