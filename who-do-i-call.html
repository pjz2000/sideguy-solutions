Who Do I Call?

If you’re asking this question, you’re not alone.

A lot of problems don’t come with clear instructions. Something breaks. Something stops working. You know it’s a problem — but you don’t know what type of professional actually handles it.

This guide is designed to help San Diego homeowners and businesses figure out the right next step before wasting time, money, or energy.

Why This Is Confusing (and Completely Normal)

Most people waste time and money because they call the wrong person first. Many companies won’t tell you if they’re not the right fit — they’ll just take the call.

SideGuy Solutions exists to help you pause, understand your options, and make a smarter first decision.
