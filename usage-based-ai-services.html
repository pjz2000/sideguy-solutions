Usage-Based AI Services

Usage-based AI services are billed based on actual usage. If nothing runs, nothing is charged.

How this differs from subscriptions

Subscriptions bill continuously. Usage-based services align cost with completed work.

When usage-based models make sense

  • Irregular workloads
  • One-off automation needs
  • Careful testing before committing

Clarity before cost

You should understand what runs and why before paying for it.
