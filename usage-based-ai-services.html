Usage-Based AI Services

Usage-based AI services charge only when a task or service is used. Costs scale with actual activity, not time.

What problem this solves

Fixed subscriptions often charge regardless of value delivered. Usage-based models align cost with use.

How usage-based services work

  • Work is measured per task or job
  • Costs apply only when work runs
  • No charge when idle

Where humans still matter

Humans decide what should run and review outputs when stakes are high.

Who this is for (and not for)

Best for uneven workloads. Not ideal for high-volume constant work.
