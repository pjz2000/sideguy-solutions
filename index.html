Why Solana?

Solana's 65,000 TPS and sub-second finality make it ideal for DeFi. Low fees (<$0.01/tx) and Rust-based smart contracts enable fast, secure apps. TVL hit $15B in 2025, with integrations like Jupiter and Raydium powering 80% of swaps.

  • High throughput: 65K TPS vs Ethereum's 15
  • Low costs: $0.00025 average fee
  • Composable: Build on SPL tokens and Anchor framework

Key Integrations

We specialize in Phantom wallet connects, Jupiter DEX routing, and Kamino lending. Example: Auto-swap SOL to USDC via Raydium, then stake in Jito for 10% APY.

  • DEX: Jupiter (aggregator), Raydium (AMM), Orca (CLMM)
  • Lending: Kamino (yield farming), Solend (flash loans)
  • Staking: Jito (MEV rewards), Marinade (mSOL LSTs)
  • Bridges: Wormhole (ETH-SOL), Allbridge (multi-chain)
