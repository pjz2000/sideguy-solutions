Future signals · 2025 and beyond
A few currents worth paying attention to.
This isn’t “financial advice” or doom scroll fodder. It’s a living snapshot of the tech waves that actually matter for North County builders.
Signal · AI as background infrastructure
The loud part of AI is the headlines. The quiet part is intake forms, routing, scheduling, follow-up, and a thousand tiny flows happening in the background. That’s where operators win.
Practical takeaway: start with one boring workflow.
Signal · Stablecoins & instant settlement
Fees, delays, and chargebacks are real friction for small businesses. Stablecoins on fast chains like Solana are quietly becoming the “back alley” rails for serious operators.
Practical takeaway: run a tiny pilot, not a rebrand.
Signal · Experiences > ads
The cost of attention is up. The value of a great experience — on-site or online — is up more. Ticketing, membership, and community-layer tools are the leverage.
Practical takeaway: design one “wow” moment into your flow.
Signal · Small, weird, local wins
The next decade belongs to people who combine weird, specific, local superpowers with sharp tools. That’s the whole SideGuy thesis.
Practical takeaway: your “too weird to scale” idea might be your edge.
