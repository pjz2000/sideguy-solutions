Blockchains are the backbone of a decentralized economy: CBDCs in 100+ countries by 2025, World Bank tracking funds on-chain, and protocol economies enabling peer-to-peer trade without intermediaries. From supply chains (IBM Food Trust) to global payments (Ripple/XRP), blockchain cuts costs 40-60% and boosts transparency. By 2030, 10% of global GDP ($10T) could be tokenized on chains like Solana and Ethereum, per McKinsey.

  • Central bank digital currencies (CBDCs): 86 countries piloting, integrating with DeFi for programmable money
  • Supply chain: IBM/Walmart track goods end-to-end, cutting fraud 30%
  • Identity & voting: Self-sovereign IDs on blockchain for secure global elections
