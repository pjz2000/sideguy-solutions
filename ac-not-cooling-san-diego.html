AC Not Cooling?

When your air is running but not cooling, the cause is usually simple — or expensive if ignored.

Common Causes

  • Low refrigerant or leaks
  • Dirty condenser or coils
  • Frozen evaporator
  • Failing capacitor or compressor
  • Incorrect thermostat settings

Why It Matters

  • Running hot systems burn out compressors
  • Energy bills spike fast
  • Small issues become full replacements
  • Heat waves amplify damage
