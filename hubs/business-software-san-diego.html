Common questions

What's the best booking software for a small service business?

Depends on your workflow. Acuity and Calendly handle simple scheduling; Square Appointments or Jobber work better for field service. Start with your biggest friction point — missed calls, no-shows, or manual scheduling — and pick a tool that solves that specific thing.

How do I choose business software without getting sold something I don't need?

List the one problem causing the most friction right now. Budget $0–150/month, try 2 options with free trials, and pay attention to whether setup takes hours or weeks. If a vendor needs a demo call before showing pricing, walk away.

My software isn't working — what do I check first?

Start with browser/cache clear, then check the vendor status page (most have one), then their active support tickets. 80% of 'software broken' reports are connectivity or browser issues. If it's been down more than 4 hours and their status page says 'all good', that's a red flag.
