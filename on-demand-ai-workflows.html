On-Demand AI Workflows

On-demand AI workflows are groups of tasks that only run when triggered. They don’t stay active in the background and don’t generate costs when idle.

Why this model exists

Many workflows are needed occasionally, not constantly. On-demand systems give businesses more control over when work runs.

Common examples

  • One-time data cleanup
  • Document processing
  • Internal reporting
  • Follow-up tasks

Human oversight

Workflows automate steps, but people still review outputs and handle edge cases.
