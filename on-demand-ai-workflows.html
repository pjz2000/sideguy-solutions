On-Demand AI Workflows

On-demand AI workflows run only when needed. They do not operate continuously and do not charge unless work is triggered.

What problem this solves

Many teams need automation only at specific moments. On-demand workflows avoid paying for idle systems.

How on-demand workflows work

  • An event or request triggers the workflow
  • The AI completes a defined process
  • The workflow stops when finished

Where humans still matter

Humans design workflows, approve results, and handle exceptions.

Who this is for (and not for)

Helpful for sporadic tasks. Less useful for always-on operations.
