Human vs Agent Workflows

The best systems combine humans and agents instead of replacing one with the other.

What problem this solves

Over-automation often removes accountability.

How responsibilities are split

  • Agents handle repeatable tasks
  • Humans handle judgment and exceptions

Where humans stay involved

Approval, escalation, and decision-making.

When this model makes sense

Operational work with clear boundaries.

When it does not

Work that depends on trust or nuance.
