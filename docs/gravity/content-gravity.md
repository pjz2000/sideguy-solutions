# SideGuy Content Gravity Engine

Purpose:

Accelerate indexing and authority for new pages.

When new pages are created they should immediately
connect to strong internal pages.

--------------------------------------------------

PROCESS

1. Detect new pages
2. Link them to knowledge hubs
3. Link them to command center
4. Link hubs back to the new pages

--------------------------------------------------

WHY THIS MATTERS

Internal linking distributes authority across the platform.

New pages gain trust faster when connected to
existing high-authority pages.

--------------------------------------------------

GOAL

Turn SideGuy into a highly interconnected knowledge system.
