# Core Mental Models

Energy Drives Civilization
More available energy increases productivity, wealth,
and technological capability.

Information Compounds
Knowledge systems grow exponentially when organized well.

Automation Multiplies Output
Robotics and software allow a single operator to achieve
the output of many workers.

Decision Clarity Saves Money
Most costly mistakes happen because decisions are rushed
or poorly understood.

Technology Diffusion
New technologies follow a pattern:

Discovery
Early adoption
Infrastructure build-out
Mass adoption

SideGuy helps people navigate that curve.
