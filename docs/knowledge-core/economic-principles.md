# Economic Principles

Abundance Through Technology
As automation and energy improve, the marginal cost
of many goods trends toward zero.

Coordination Becomes Valuable
When information is abundant, trusted coordination
and guidance become scarce and valuable.

Markets Allocate Resources
Digital payment systems and market mechanisms
coordinate production across the economy.

Human Judgment Remains Critical
Even with advanced AI systems,
humans still interpret context, ethics,
and trade-offs.
