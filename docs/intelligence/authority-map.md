# SideGuy Authority Map

Purpose:

Provide visibility into the strength and structure of the SideGuy platform.

The authority map identifies:

- strongest topic clusters
- weak clusters
- orphan pages
- hub coverage

This allows SideGuy to continuously strengthen the platform.

--------------------------------------------------

METRICS

Pages per topic
Pages missing hub links
Pages missing command center links
Pages missing FAQ schema
Thin pages

--------------------------------------------------

GOAL

Turn SideGuy into a continuously improving knowledge platform.
