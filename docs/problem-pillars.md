# SideGuy Problem Pillars

These are the five primary problem categories that drive the majority of small business search traffic.

---

## 1. Money Problems

Examples:

- payment processor fees too high
- reduce credit card fees
- POS systems
- chargeback prevention
- payment automation

---

## 2. Automation Problems

Examples:

- AI phone answering
- scheduling automation
- AI customer support
- workflow automation

---

## 3. Customer Acquisition Problems

Examples:

- why am I not getting leads
- Google ads help
- SEO for contractors
- website traffic problems

---

## 4. Operations Problems

Examples:

- employee scheduling software
- inventory management
- CRM systems
- project management

---

## 5. Technology Confusion

Examples:

- which AI tools should I use
- what CRM is best
- which payment processor should I choose
- how to automate a business

---
