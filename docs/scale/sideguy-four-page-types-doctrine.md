# SideGuy Four Page Types Doctrine

Scaling to a million pages only works if pages fall into a small number of repeatable structures.

The page is not the product.

The **cluster** is the product.

---

## The 4 Page Types

### 1. Hub Pages

Purpose: Primary authority pages.

Traits:
- curated
- strong copy
- internal link destination
- trust blocks
- FAQ schema
- human-first explanation

Examples:

machine-to-machine-payments.html  
solana-payments.html  
ai-automation.html  
san-diego-hvac-help.html

Rule:

Hubs are curated.

Never mass generate hubs.

---

### 2. Supporting Problem Pages

Purpose:

Answer specific questions that reinforce a hub.

Traits:

- narrow intent
- templatable
- long-tail search traffic
- links to hub

Examples:

can-ai-agents-pay-for-apis.html  
why-is-my-ac-blowing-warm-air.html  
how-does-programmable-money-work.html  

Rule:

Every problem page must have:

- one parent hub
- one clear intent
- real explanation
- internal link logic

---

### 3. Local Variant Pages

Purpose:

Connect topics to cities and service areas.

Traits:

- localized framing
- genuine local relevance
- not doorway spam

Examples:

ai-automation-san-diego.html  
solana-payments-north-county.html  
hvac-decision-help-encinitas.html  

Rule:

Only build when local intent exists.

---

### 4. Comparison / Decision Pages

Purpose:

Help users choose between options.

Traits:

- high commercial intent
- bridge between education and action

Examples:

stripe-vs-usdc-payments.html  
ai-chatbot-vs-human-support.html  
solana-vs-traditional-payment-rails.html  

Rule:

Comparison pages are **money pages**.

Treat them carefully.

---

## Million Page Model

Curated hubs  
+ scaled problem pages  
+ selective local variants  
+ decision pages  

= controlled topical authority.

---

## SideGuy Quality Rules

Every page must have:

- clear topic
- clear intent
- human explanation
- internal links
- trust section
- next step
- Text PJ orb

---

## Anti-Spam Rules

Do not:

- generate fake city pages
- duplicate pages with keyword swaps
- orphan pages without hubs

Do:

- strengthen hubs
- expand real problems
- review clusters

---

## One-Line Model

Curated hubs.  
Scaled problem pages.  
Real local variants.  
High-intent comparison pages.
