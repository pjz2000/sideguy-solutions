# SideGuy Internal Link Multiplier

Purpose:

Strengthen the internal linking structure of the SideGuy platform.

Large knowledge sites grow authority by ensuring pages link to:

- their knowledge hub
- the command center
- related pages
- the homepage

This multiplies link equity and helps search engines understand the site architecture.

--------------------------------------------------

RULES

Every page should link to:

homepage  
command center  
at least one knowledge hub  
at least two related pages  

--------------------------------------------------

WHY THIS MATTERS

Internal linking improves:

crawl efficiency  
topic authority  
ranking potential  

--------------------------------------------------

SIDEGUY STRUCTURE

Homepage

↓

Command Center

↓

Knowledge Hubs

↓

Explainer Pages

↓

Problem Pages

--------------------------------------------------

GOAL

Turn SideGuy into a highly interconnected knowledge platform.
