# SideGuy Money Philosophy

Traditional response to money decay:

save cash
wait
lose purchasing power

Better response:

build productive systems
create useful knowledge
increase trust
generate opportunities
move value faster

Three levels of response:

1. Personal
Turn skills, time, and attention into productive assets.

2. Business
Build evergreen lead flow and service infrastructure.

3. Infrastructure
Support better rails such as instant settlement,
programmable payments, and stablecoin-based systems.

Key line:

Do not just hold money.
Build systems that can outrun decay.
