# SideGuy Authority Doctrine

Authority is not a vibe.

Authority is structure.

Search engines and AI systems trust sites that demonstrate:

- topic coverage

- internal consistency

- repeated relevance

- clear hierarchy

- useful pages across the decision journey

SideGuy builds authority by mapping real-world problem spaces and reinforcing them with clusters, local pages, predicted-intent pages, and human resolution.

topic

## What authority is made of

1. Coverage

   - enough pages to show the topic is real

   - enough subtopics to show depth



2. Structure

   - hubs

   - child pages

   - cluster pages

   - local pages

   - supporting explainers



3. Internal linking

   - page to hub

   - hub to page

   - sibling support

   - related cluster crossover



4. Consistency

   - repeated naming patterns

   - repeatable templates

   - clean paths

   - stable architecture



5. Human trust layer

   - clear explanations

   - no hype

   - no spammy chaos

   - real human next step
