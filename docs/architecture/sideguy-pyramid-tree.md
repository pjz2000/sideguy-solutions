# SideGuy Pyramid-Tree Architecture (Canonical)

Last updated: 2026-03-05 16:54:37

## Why this exists
SideGuy is not "a blog." SideGuy is a **Problem Intelligence Infrastructure**:
- Google discovers the problem
- AI explains it clearly
- A real human resolves it (Text PJ)

This doc locks the **pyramid + tree** site architecture:
**ROOT (identity) → TRUNK (pillars) → BRANCHES (clusters) → LEAVES (long-tail pages)**

---

## 1) The Root (Site Identity)
The ROOT is the consistent identity across every page:
- **Clarity before cost**
- Human-first problem solving
- AI explanation → Human resolution
- Text PJ is always available (authenticity + conversion)

**ROOT must appear subtly on every page** (one short line or micro-block).

---

## 2) The Trunk (Authority Pillars)
PILLARS are major domains SideGuy can own. Target ~10 initially.

Each pillar page:
- Defines the domain
- Lists the main clusters under it
- Links to cluster pages
- Reinforces ROOT identity

Pillars are like "section homepages" (high internal link equity).

---

## 3) The Branches (Clusters)
CLUSTERS organize topics within a pillar. Target 20–50 clusters per pillar over time.

Each cluster page:
- Explains the topic in friendly language
- Lists common problems/questions
- Links out to leaf pages
- Links back up to pillar
- Links sideways to related clusters

Clusters are where **Google trust** forms (topical authority).

---

## 4) The Leaves (Long-Tail Pages)
LEAVES are programmatic pages targeting narrow queries.

Every leaf page should:
- Link UP to its cluster
- Link UP to its pillar
- Link SIDEWAYS to 2–5 related leaf pages (same cluster)
- Include a "Friendly Intelligence" block:
  - Quick answer
  - Why this happens
  - What people misunderstand
  - Simple next steps
  - When to text PJ

---

## Internal Linking Rules (Non-Negotiable)
Every page needs "upward and sideways" links:

### Leaf page
- Must include:
  - `Back to Cluster`
  - `Back to Pillar`
  - `Related Pages` (same cluster)
  - `Text PJ` CTA

### Cluster page
- Must include:
  - `Back to Pillar`
  - `Top leaf pages` list
  - `Related clusters` list
  - `Text PJ` CTA

### Pillar page
- Must include:
  - `Top clusters` list
  - `Featured leaf pages` list
  - `Related pillars` list
  - `Text PJ` CTA

---

## Friendly Intelligence Template (Tone)
SideGuy must sound like:
**a smart friend explaining things calmly**.

Bad: "Thermodynamic systems operate…"
SideGuy: "Your AC compressor is basically the heart of the system…"

---

## The Intelligence Loop
Over time:
Search demand → pages rank → GSC queries reveal needs → generate deeper cluster + leaf pages.

This is "Problem Radar":
- Build where impressions/clicks are emerging.
- Don't guess forever; respond to real demand.

---

## Success Metrics (Phase 2)
- More clusters/pillars with internal link density
- Higher % of pages with proper "up links"
- Improving impressions distribution across clusters
- Conversions to Text PJ from multiple entry points
