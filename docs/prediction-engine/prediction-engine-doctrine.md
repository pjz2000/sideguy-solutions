# SideGuy Prediction Engine Doctrine

SideGuy does not wait for search demand.

SideGuy predicts it.

---

Core Principle:

Every page = a predicted question.

---

The system:

intent (what user is trying to solve)

→ probability (what they are likely to search)

→ page (pre-built answer)

→ human resolution (Text PJ)

---

We do not chase keywords.

We map:

- problems

- decisions

- confusion points

- future questions

---

Prediction Layers:

1. Known demand

   - existing search queries



2. Emerging demand

   - new tech

   - new tools

   - news



3. Latent demand

   - questions users don't know how to ask yet

---

Goal:

Be the most probable answer

before the search is fully formed.
