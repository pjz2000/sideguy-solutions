# SideGuy Trend Radar

Purpose:

Identify emerging technology and infrastructure topics
before they become mainstream searches.

SideGuy publishes early explainers for new concepts.

--------------------------------------------------

EXAMPLES

AI agents for business  
machine to machine payments  
autonomous energy trading  
robot delivery infrastructure  
stablecoin payroll  

--------------------------------------------------

PROCESS

1. Add new signals to trend-signals.txt
2. Radar engine identifies strong topics
3. Problem engine generates pages
4. Pages connect to knowledge hubs

--------------------------------------------------

GOAL

SideGuy becomes the first place explaining new systems.
