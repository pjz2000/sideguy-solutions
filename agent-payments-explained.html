Agent Payments Explained

Agent payments describe a model where specific tasks are paid for only when they run and complete.

What this usually means

Work is broken into clear jobs with defined outcomes instead of open-ended billing.

What happens next

Most tasks complete normally. Occasionally a human review is needed. Rarely, work is stopped and adjusted.

When a human gets involved

Humans approve results, handle exceptions, and make judgment calls.

When not to use this

Constant, high-trust, or ambiguous work.

Risks and limits

Over-automation can remove accountability if humans are not involved.
