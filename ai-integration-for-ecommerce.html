Where operators get stuck

Customer support is a manual bottleneck

For most ecommerce operators, 70–80% of support tickets are the same 10 questions. AI-trained support automation can deflect this volume while routing edge cases to humans.

Inventory and reorder decisions are guesswork

AI demand forecasting tools integrate with most ecommerce platforms and significantly reduce both stockouts and overstock. Most operators don’t know these tools exist at their price point.

Email and SMS flows are set-and-ignore

Most ecommerce operators have Klaviyo or similar but are using 20% of its capability. AI-assisted segmentation and flow optimization can materially improve LTV without new tools.
