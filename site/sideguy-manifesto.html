The SideGuy Manifesto

Updated: 2026-01-13

SideGuy exists because the internet is good at creating options — and bad at helping people decide.

What SideGuy Is

  • A clarity layer
  • A translation layer
  • A human-in-the-loop system
  • A pause before decisions

What SideGuy Is Not

  • A course
  • An agency
  • A hype machine
  • A marketplace

The Belief

Clarity before cost.

Better questions beat faster answers.

Calm beats panic.

If you’re unsure what to do next, that’s the moment SideGuy is for.
