AC Making Noise in San Diego?

Rattling, buzzing, banging, or humming louder than normal usually means something is wrong — and guessing can turn a small issue into an expensive failure. SideGuy helps you understand what’s happening before anyone sells you a system.

Common AC Noises

  • Banging or clanking (loose or broken parts)
  • Buzzing (electrical or debris issues)
  • Screeching (motor or belt problems)
  • Rattling panels or duct noise

Who This Is For

  • San Diego homeowners
  • Property managers
  • Restaurants & offices
  • Commercial buildings

Why It Matters

  • Prevent full system failure
  • Avoid emergency downtime
  • Protect compressors & motors
