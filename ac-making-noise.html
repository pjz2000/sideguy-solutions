Quick Checks That Work for Any Problem

Before calling anyone or panicking, run these four universal checks:

  • Identify when the issue started (before/after weather, guests, changes, installs).
  • Look for anything unusual: leaks, smells, sounds, flashes, sparks, or heat.
  • Try safe resets: breakers, switches, routers, valves, thermostats, GFCI outlets.
  • Take a photo or 15-second video — it instantly speeds up diagnosis.

These steps solve 20–40% of issues immediately and give PJ everything needed to guide you.

Urgency Check
Signs You Should Text PJ Immediately
  • Active water leak or anything dripping where it shouldn’t be.
  • Burning smell, warm outlets, buzzing panels, or flickering lights.
  • No heat or hot water during cold nights.
  • Any gas smell or “rotten egg” odor.

If you’re unsure, assume it’s urgent → then scale down if it’s not.
