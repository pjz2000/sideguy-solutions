Why AC systems start making noise

Noisy AC systems are often early warning signs. Loose parts, failing motors, airflow restrictions, or compressor stress can all show up as sound before full system failure.

  • Banging or clanking (loose or broken components)
  • Buzzing (electrical or capacitor issues)
  • Screeching or squealing (motor or belt problems)
  • Rattling panels or duct vibration
  • Hissing (airflow or refrigerant-related issues)
