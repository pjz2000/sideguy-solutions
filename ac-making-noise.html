SideGuy Problem Fix Guide

This simple guide helps you understand the issue, try safe steps, and get a real human fast.

Quick Checks

  • See when the issue started.
  • Look for leaks, smells, noises, or anything unusual.
  • Try safe resets (breaker, switch, GFCI, thermostat, valve).
  • Take a photo or 10–20 sec video.

Urgent Signs

  • Active water leak or flooding
  • Burning smell or smoke
  • Sparks or buzzing electrical panel
  • Total loss of heat/hot water in cold conditions

If anything seems unsafe, stop and text PJ immediately.
