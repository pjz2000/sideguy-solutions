AC Making Noise? Real HVAC Help for San Diego Homes & Businesses

If your air conditioner is rattling, buzzing, banging, screeching, or humming louder than normal, something is wrong — and ignoring it usually makes repairs more expensive.

Problem: AC systems making unusual noise often signal loose parts, failing motors, airflow issues, or compressor trouble.

Location: San Diego County — residential and commercial properties.

Who This Is For: Homeowners, property managers, restaurants, offices, and facilities teams.

What Happens Next: You talk to a real human who helps you understand the issue and connect with the right fix.

Common AC Noises (and What They Usually Mean)

  • Banging or clanking: Loose or broken internal components
  • Buzzing: Electrical issues, failing contactors, or debris
  • Screeching: Motor or belt problems
  • Rattling: Panels, fasteners, or duct issues
  • Hissing: Possible refrigerant or airflow problems

Why This Matters More for Commercial Buildings

  • Noise often precedes system failure
  • Downtime impacts customers, staff, and tenants
  • Emergency repairs cost more than early fixes
  • Older rooftop units hide problems until they escalate
