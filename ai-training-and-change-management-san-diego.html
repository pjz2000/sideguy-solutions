Training & change management — help your team adopt AI

Change management keeps pilots from failing after launch: training, role clarity, and clear communication to reduce resistance and confusion.

Training checklist

  • Run short hands-on sessions showing goals and tools
  • Provide written runbooks and sample inputs/outputs
  • Define who handles exceptions and escalation

Communication

Be transparent about what changes and why—explain augmentation goals so staff understands the benefit and new responsibilities.

Measure adoption

Track adoption metrics: frequency of use, error rates, and human override rates. Use the data to refine training and tooling.

FAQ

What’s the right training cadence?
Short, repeated sessions work best—start with an intro and follow up after a week with hands-on troubleshooting.
