PayPal Business Account Won't Verify? Here's How to Fix It

PayPal's business verification fails for 4 main reasons: name mismatches, bank linking errors, document rejections, or mystery "under review" holds. If you've been stuck for more than 2 days, one of these is your blocker. Here's how to clear each one.

⚡ Quick Diagnostic Checklist

Run through these first:

  • Does your PayPal business name match your EIN letter exactly? (Including "LLC" vs "L.L.C.")
  • Are you using a business checking account, not a personal account?
  • Is your uploaded document recent (under 90 days), clear, and showing your business address?
  • Have you checked spam for PayPal's verification emails?

If all check out and you're still stuck → call 1-888-883-9770 and ask for escalation.

Most Common Setup Blockers

1. "We couldn't verify your identity"

Why this happens: Name mismatch between PayPal registration and legal business documents. Even middle initials or LLC punctuation differences trigger rejections.

Fix:

  • Check your exact legal business name on your EIN confirmation letter or Articles of Organization
  • Update your PayPal profile to match exactly — including commas, periods, and "LLC" vs "L.L.C."
  • If you're a sole proprietor using your personal name, make sure it matches your SSN records, not a DBA

2. Bank account won't link or verify

Why this happens: PayPal makes two small deposits (under $1) to verify ownership. If those deposits don't appear within 2-3 business days, the link fails.

Fix:

  • Use a business checking account, not a personal account linked to your business profile
  • Call your bank first — some flag PayPal deposits as suspicious and block them automatically
  • If you're using an online bank (Relay, Mercury, Novo), verification usually works faster than traditional banks
  • Critical: Don't retry more than twice. Each failed attempt adds a 72-hour lockout

3. Document upload keeps getting rejected

Why this happens: PayPal requires proof of business existence — usually an EIN letter, utility bill, or bank statement. If the document is too old, blurry, or doesn't show your registered business address, it gets rejected.

Fix:

  • Documents must be dated within the last 90 days (IRS EIN letters are exempt from this rule)
  • Upload PDF or JPG only — no screenshots, no photos of screens
  • Make sure your business name and address are clearly visible
  • If you operate from home, use your registered business address, not a P.O. box

4. Account stuck "under review" for days

Why this happens: PayPal's fraud detection flags new business accounts, especially if your IP address, device, or business structure looks unusual. There's no transparency into what triggers this.

Fix:

  • Call PayPal Business support at 1-888-883-9770 — the automated system is useless; ask for escalation
  • Have your EIN, formation date, and business bank info ready
  • If they ask for a "letter of explanation," write 2-3 sentences describing your business and why you need the account — formal tone, no fluff
  • Some reviews can take 5-10 business days with no way to speed them up

⚠️ If nothing is working

Sometimes PayPal's system just rejects your business type or industry without clear reason. If you've been blocked three times, trying again won't help. Consider Stripe or Square instead — both have faster verification and clearer rejection reasons.

Should You Even Use PayPal Business?

PayPal still dominates for eBay sellers, international transactions, and buyers who don't trust smaller brands with their credit card. But if you're running a local San Diego service business, restaurant, or shop, Square or Stripe will cause you less friction. PayPal's holds, disputes, and sudden account freezes create cash flow problems that other processors don't. If you've been stuck for 5+ days with no progress, switching platforms is often faster than waiting for PayPal support.

Common Questions

How long does PayPal Business verification take?

Usually 1-3 business days if all your documents are correct. Can stretch to 10+ days if flagged for manual review. No way to speed it up.

Can I use my personal PayPal account for business?

Technically yes, but you'll hit limits fast ($10K/month receiving cap, no invoicing tools, worse fraud protection). If you're doing real volume, upgrade to Business or switch platforms entirely.

Why does PayPal keep asking for the same documents?

Their system auto-rejects documents it can't read clearly (bad scans, wrong file types, missing info). Make sure the business name and address are visible and match your profile exactly. If it keeps happening, call support — sometimes it's a glitch they need to override manually.

What if I don't have an EIN yet?

Sole proprietors can use their SSN instead. But if you're filing as an LLC or corporation, get your EIN first from irs.gov (takes 5 minutes online). PayPal won't approve corporate accounts without it.

Can I change my business name after verification? · Updated March 2026

Yes, but it requires re-verification. You'll need to upload updated formation documents showing the legal name change. Expect another 3-7 day review.
