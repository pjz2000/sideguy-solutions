How SideGuy Works

SideGuy is where Google discovers the problem, AI explains the options, and a real human helps you resolve it. Not a chatbot. Not a service marketplace. A calm operator who helps you understand before you spend.

1

Google finds the problem

You searched for something real — a broken system, a confusing process, a decision you didn't know how to make. SideGuy shows up because the page exists to answer that exact question.

2

AI explains the options

Once you're here, you get a structured explanation — what's likely happening, what the options are, what the tradeoffs look like. No upsells. No confusing jargon. Just clarity.

3

PJ helps you make the right call

When you need a real conversation, text PJ. Describe what's going on. You'll get a direct, honest response — not a sales pitch. If SideGuy isn't the right fit, we'll tell you who is.

Core Principle
Clarity before cost

Most people make their worst decisions when they're stressed and time-pressured. SideGuy exists to slow that down — not by adding friction, but by giving you just enough understanding to make a confident move. You should know what you're getting before you spend a dollar.

Why this works better than a search spiral

Search gives you noise

  • 10 competing vendors with identical claims
  • Reddit threads from 3 years ago
  • Blog posts that exist to sell you something

SideGuy gives you signal

  • Structured problem/solution pages
  • Honest tradeoffs explained plainly
  • Human confirmation available

What happens after

  • You know what to ask for
  • You know what fair pricing looks like
  • You make a confident decision, not a pressured one

Human judgment + AI structure

AI is very good at organizing information and explaining patterns. Humans are very good at reading context, catching what doesn't add up, and knowing when a situation is unusual. SideGuy uses both deliberately — AI to structure the knowledge, PJ to apply judgment.

This isn't a chatbot. You're not talking to a language model that will tell you what you want to hear. You're reaching a real operator who has worked through these problems before — in payments, software, home services, and operator workflows.
