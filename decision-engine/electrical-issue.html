Should I panic about an electrical issue?

Short answer

Usually no — but context matters.

Do NOT panic if:

  • The issue is intermittent
  • Nothing smells like burning or gas
  • No active water damage is spreading

DO act quickly if:

  • You smell gas or burning
  • There is active flooding
  • Power loss affects critical systems
