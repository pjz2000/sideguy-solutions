AI pricing & packages

Typical ranges and example pricing to help you plan a pilot or a small rollout — plain language, no surprise fees.

  • Pilot (2–4 weeks): $2,500–$8,000 depending on scope
  • Fixed-scope project: starting at $8,000
  • Ongoing support: monthly retainers or pay-as-you-go

How we price

We price pilots based on scope, data needs, and integration complexity. Fixed-scope projects include defined deliverables and acceptance criteria.

What’s included

  • Discovery & scoping
  • Pilot build and test
  • Runbooks and basic training

Timelines & deliverables

Pilots: 2–4 weeks to an MVP; fixed projects: typically 6–12 weeks depending on complexity.

Payment & contracts

We do 50% upfront for pilots and the remainder on delivery; retainers are billed monthly with a simple cancellation policy.

Example scenarios

  • Auto-response pilot for customer messages — pilot: $3,500
  • Invoice automation & routing — pilot: $4,500
  • Simple inventory forecasting — pilot: $6,000
