Pay-Per-Use AI Jobs

Pay-per-use AI jobs are tasks that only cost money when they actually run and complete. There are no monthly retainers and no ongoing fees if nothing happens.

What problem this solves

Many businesses pay for software they barely use. Pay-per-use jobs reduce waste by charging only when real work is done.

How pay-per-use jobs usually work

  • A specific task is defined
  • The job runs only when triggered
  • Payment happens after completion

Where humans still matter

Humans define the task, review outcomes, and step in when judgment or accountability is required.

Who this is for (and not for)

This works best for irregular or outcome-based work. It is not ideal for constant daily operations or subjective decisions.
