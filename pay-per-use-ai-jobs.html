Pay-Per-Use AI Jobs (Explained Simply)

Pay-per-use AI jobs are small, on-demand tasks that run only when you need them. Instead of monthly subscriptions or retainers, you pay only when a specific job runs and completes.

What problem this solves

Many businesses pay for tools they rarely use. Pay-per-use jobs reduce waste by aligning cost directly with real work.

How pay-per-use jobs work

  • A task is triggered intentionally
  • The job runs once, on demand
  • Payment happens only after completion

Where humans still matter

Automation handles repeatable steps. Humans provide judgment, context, and oversight when decisions matter.
