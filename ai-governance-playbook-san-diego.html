AI governance playbook — simple policies and roles

Practical governance guidance for small teams: how to assign ownership, set policies, and respond to incidents without a heavyweight process.

Core roles

  • Owner (business lead)
  • Technical lead (ops/engineer)
  • Reviewer (privacy/security/qa)

Policies to define

  • Data retention and access rules
  • Model review criteria and versioning
  • Failure and rollback procedures

Incident response

Keep a short incident playbook: identify, contain, investigate, and remediate. Document outcomes and add learning items to the next sprint.

FAQ

Do small teams need a governance committee?
No—start with named roles and a lightweight review process; scale governance as you scale deployments.
