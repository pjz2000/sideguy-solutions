What should I know about payment processing in San Diego?

Payment processing in San Diego works the same as anywhere, but local businesses often overpay due to confusing contracts. The typical effective rate for card-present transactions should be 2.0–2.9%. If you're above that, it's worth a quick review. Text PJ with your current monthly volume and processor name — most businesses identify savings within a week.

How do I choose the right payment processing company in San Diego?

Start with volume: under $5k/month, use Square free tier. $5k–$15k, evaluate stripe or Clover with interchange-plus pricing. Over $15k/month, negotiate a local merchant account — you should be paying closer to 1.5–1.8% effective rate. Avoid long-term contracts until you've run a 30-day trial.
