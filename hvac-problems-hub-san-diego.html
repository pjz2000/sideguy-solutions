🏠 When to Call vs. DIY

Try DIY first: Changing filters, checking breakers, adjusting thermostat, cleaning around outdoor unit.

Call a pro: No cooling after filter change, strange noises, burning smells, electrical issues, refrigerant leaks.

Emergency (call now): Gas smell, electrical sparking, complete winter heating loss, smoke.
