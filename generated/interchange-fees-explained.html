🚀 Fast Checklist

  1. Read your processing statementLook for the effective rate line — that's your real cost.
  2. Identify the fee layersInterchange is fixed. Processor markup is negotiable.
  3. Compare your rate to benchmarksMost small businesses should be under 2.7% effective rate.
  4. Flag any surprisesPCI fees, batch fees, and monthly minimums are the hidden ones.
  5. Decide: optimize or switchSmall tweaks can save hundreds/month before switching makes sense.
