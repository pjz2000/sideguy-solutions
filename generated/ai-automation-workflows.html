🚀 Fast Checklist

  1. Map the current manual processWrite out each step you do today — this becomes your automation spec.
  2. Pick your triggerAutomations start with a trigger: new lead, missed call, form submit, etc.
  3. Connect your existing toolsMost automations work with Zapier, Make.com, or a native integration.
  4. Test with 10 real interactionsSmall batch first — real data reveals edge cases that demos hide.
  5. Measure and iterateTrack the metric this automation was supposed to move — check weekly.
