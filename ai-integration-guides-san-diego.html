AI integration guides — Zapier, Airtable, CRMs, and more

Concrete steps and patterns to integrate AI pilots into existing workflows without heavy engineering work.

Common patterns

  • Trigger → AI process → action (e.g., new message → classify → route)
  • Batch processing for non-real-time tasks (e.g., nightly forecasting)
  • Human-in-the-loop verification before final actions

Zapier & no-code

Use Zapier or Make to wire small automations and keep changes visible to business users. Great for quick pilots that need minimal engineering.

Spreadsheets & Airtable

Use sheets as a control plane for sampling, labeling, and simple workflows; store inputs and outputs for traceability during pilots.

CRM & ticketing systems

Integrate classification and routing with tickets to reduce manual triage. Keep a summary of the AI decision in the ticket for auditing.

FAQ

How do we debug integrations?
Record inputs/outputs and use replay testing to reproduce issues; keep a separate staging environment for integrations where possible.
