When to pilot

Start with a small, measurable use case — customer messages, scheduling, or basic admin tasks where outcomes are easy to measure.

Typical process

  • Discovery & scoping (1 week)
  • Pilot build & test (2–4 weeks)
  • Measure, iterate, then scale

Data & privacy

We minimize sensitive data during pilots, document data flows, and recommend retention and access controls that fit your risk profile.

Integration & handoff

We deliver runnable automations, clear runbooks, and training so your team owns the workflow after handoff.

Success metrics

Define measurable outcomes (time saved, accuracy, or revenue impact) and check them at pilot milestones.
