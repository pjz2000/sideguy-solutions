About PJ

SideGuy is run by PJ — an operator with a background spanning hospitality, tech, payments, and real-world problem solving. No corporate bio. No polished agency speak. Just a real person who built this because it needed to exist.

Before SideGuy, PJ spent years inside operational environments where things break — hospitality environments where the HVAC goes down on a Saturday, software rollouts that miss by a mile, payment processors who disappear with your float. You learn to navigate systems under pressure. You learn who to trust and who to avoid.

SideGuy was built from that experience. The site exists because most people searching for help with a hard decision are one misstep away from an expensive mistake — and the internet isn't structured to stop that. It's structured to sell them something.

PJ doesn't run an agency. Doesn't have a sales team. Doesn't have a quota. When you text, you get a direct response from someone who has actually worked through the type of problem you're describing.

If SideGuy isn't the right fit for your situation, you'll hear that clearly — along with a better direction to move.
