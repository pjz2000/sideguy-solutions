HVAC Repair vs Replace in Escondido — What to Actually Do

Escondido is an inland city with hot summers, a growing downtown, and a mix of residential neighborhoods from older ranch properties to newer developments. When your HVAC stops working here, the decision matters.

The Real Question

You're not really asking "should I repair or replace?" You're asking "am I about to waste money?" That's the right question.

Why Escondido Is Different

Escondido regularly hits 95-105°F in summer — this is real heat, not coastal 80s. Your HVAC works harder here than anywhere else in the county.

Most common issue: system can't keep up on triple-digit days — often means the unit is undersized for the heat load or refrigerant is low.

Cost reality in Escondido: $300–$900 for repairs; $5,500–$12,000 for replacement (higher end because of heat load requirements).

When to Repair

  • System is under 10 years old
  • Problem is isolated (one component, not systemic)
  • Repair cost is less than 40% of replacement cost
  • No strange smells, no refrigerant leaks, no electrical issues

When to Replace

  • System is 15+ years old
  • You've had 2+ repairs in the past 12 months
  • Energy bills keep climbing despite maintenance
  • System uses R-22 refrigerant (phased out — refills are expensive)

Pro Tip for Escondido

In Escondido heat, a 2-ton unit in a 2,000 sq ft home won't cut it. You likely need 3.5-4 tons. If a contractor quotes replacement, make sure they do a Manual J load calculation — not just matching the old unit's size..

Escondido local note: Escondido's older homes near downtown often have no insulation in the attic. Adding blown-in insulation ($1,500-2,500) can reduce your cooling costs by 25-40% and extend your HVAC system's life by years.

What Most People Get Wrong

They get one quote and panic. Get two opinions minimum. A good HVAC company will explain what's wrong without pressuring you to replace. If someone shows up and immediately says "you need a whole new system" without running diagnostics, get a second opinion.
