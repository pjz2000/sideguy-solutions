ai driven compliance systems

Compliance-as-Code means treating compliance as logic, not screenshots.

Instead of logging into software, businesses define:

  • Controls as rules
  • Evidence as data
  • Audits as timelines

AI makes this practical for small and mid-sized teams.

This does not replace lawyers or auditors.

It replaces spreadsheets, checklists, and vendor lock-in.

How This Connects to Ownership

Compliance-as-Code is usually owned, versioned, and reusable.

That is why ownership becomes a real option.
