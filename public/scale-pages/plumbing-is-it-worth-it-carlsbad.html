What's the real cost comparison?
Don't just compare the quote you got. Factor in future repair probability, energy efficiency delta, and expected lifespan of current equipment.

Who gave you that number?
Contractors in Carlsbad have financial incentive toward one answer. Get a second opinion, ideally from someone with no stake in what you decide.

The 50% Rule of Thumb

In the trades, there's a general guide: if repair cost exceeds 50% of replacement cost and the equipment is past 60% of its expected lifespan, replacement usually wins on a 5-year cost basis. But this varies significantly by equipment condition, brand reliability, and local Carlsbad labor rates. It's a starting point, not a verdict.
