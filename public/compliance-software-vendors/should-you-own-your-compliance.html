Should You Own Your Compliance System?

This page is not advocating for or against SaaS tools.

It exists to help you think clearly about tradeoffs.

Renting Compliance Software Works When

  • You want speed over control
  • Compliance scope is narrow
  • Licensing cost is acceptable

Owning Compliance Systems Is Considered When

  • Compliance repeats year after year
  • Multiple entities reuse the same logic
  • Cost compounds over time
  • You want portability and control

Neither approach is wrong.

The mistake is not understanding the difference.
